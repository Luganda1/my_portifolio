I grew up in a small town in Uganda and all my life i admired to be a tech savvy, i only watched it on TV and always was fascinated with the way they created such high tech movies and how they came up with ideas and more so movies that had hacking scenes. Being from third world country i had no access to such knowledge.

When i moved out to Los angeles, california a couples of years back, boy i was determined to learn how to code. I tried joining full time school and realised i had a lot of bills to pay for and didn't qualify for any grant whatsoever. But i wasn't gonna see my dream go down the drain so i learnt thru youtube and Udemy that i could teach myself how to code and started with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Reactjs, Expressjs, Nodejs, Threejs and Python, Flask. I also learned the different CMS like Shopify, WIX and WordPress
